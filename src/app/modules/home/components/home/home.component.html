What is a Conformation Show?

Dog shows, or conformation events originated as a way for breeder's to evaluate their breeding stock. Dogs at these events compete for points towards their championship. There are 3 types of events in a conformation show, these are All-Breed Shows, Specialty Shows, and Group Shows. Dogs are evaluated based on how closely they conform to their breed's official standards. The more they conform, the better their chances at winning Best in Show!
